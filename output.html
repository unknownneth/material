OUTPUT HARDWARE

any piece of computer hardware that converts information or data into a human-perceptible form or into a physical machine-readable form for use with other non-computerized equipment.

Monitor

A display screen used to present output from a computer, video camera, VCR, or other video generator.

Printer

A device that accepts text and graphic output from a computer and transfers the information to paper.

Speaker

An output port to hear the audio produced by the computer.
